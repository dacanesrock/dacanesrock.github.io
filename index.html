About Me

I'm a marketing guru by day, photographer by night, and web developer in training anytime in between. The key is to always be doing... something. Never let yourself fall behind. The second you stand still, you lose a step.

I don't believe in god or politics but I do believe in Auburn University and love it. War Eagle forever. On the weekends you'll catch me in front of the screen, decked out in burnt orange and navy blue, yelling War Eagle every chance i get.

I have a love for all art, classic or contemporary, loud or quiet, meaningful or abstract. I ride to the rhythm and let the beat carry me through my work - as in to say I really love music. I'm a film loving liberal arts major who dabbles in tech and likes to know more than you. It's not an ego thing. It's about preparation.
